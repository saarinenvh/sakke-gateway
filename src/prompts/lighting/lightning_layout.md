## Spatial Lighting Principles

- The room is narrow → light spreads easily across walls
- Strong lights affect multiple walls quickly

- Do NOT light all walls equally
- Use walls to create direction and contrast

Typical composition:
- wall_1_tv = focal / cinematic
- wall_3_sofa_desk = base + functional lighting
- wall_4_cabinet = depth and accent
- wall_2_window = soft or minimal

- Good lighting creates imbalance on purpose (directional feel)
